About HydroCav

HydroCav Inc. is a Water Enhancement and Treatment company focused on commercializing its products based on a patent-approved design using hydrodynamic cavitation technology.

The company was initially formed in New Mexico on April 29, 2019, as a single-member LLC by David Trippett, who is the inventor of the HydroPuck Multistage Cavitation System. It was re-formed as a Massachusetts entity in January 2023.

Our mission is to utilize this patented technology to create superior water quality for a variety of uses and markets, aiming to provide safe, super-hydrating water free from harmful biological and dissolved contaminants globally.

Our Technology

The HydroCav device, invented by David Trippett in 2019, has received final approval for a U.S. patent and has been filed for international patent protection.

Our patented HydroPuck Multistage Cavitation System uses hydrodynamic cavitation technology to remove heavy metals and bacteria from fluids, thus enhancing water quality.

The technology creates microscopic bubbles that implode, generating localized high temperatures and pressures that destroy biological contaminants while also improving water's physical properties.
